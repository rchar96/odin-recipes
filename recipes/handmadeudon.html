Udon from Scratch

Arguably better than store-bought frozen udon but Maria insists they are worth the effort


A basket of fresh udon

Ingredients List (per serving):

  • 400g Flour (中力粉 Flour preferred)
  • 180g Water (room temperature)
  • 17g Salt
  • Potato Starch for dusting

The recipe is as follows...

  1. Create a brine by dissolving the salt in the water
  2. Slowly add the brine solution to the flour between 3-4 pours, stirring the mixture between pours
  3. Knead the dough into a ball
  4. Continue kneading the dough 150-200 times until the dough becomes elastic
  5. Reform the dough into a ball and place it in a covered bowl to rest for 90-120 minutes
  6. Press the dough outinto laters by using a rolling pin
  7. Dust the dough with starch and wrap the dough around the rolling pin
  8. Unroll the dough and fold it like a fan
  9. Cut the dough into noodles using a knife
  10. Bring a large pot of water to boil
  11. Boil the noodles for 12-13 minutes (stirring every 30 seconds or so)
  12. Rince off the noodles in cold water to remove any excess starch
  13. Combine with the broth of your choosing and serve!
