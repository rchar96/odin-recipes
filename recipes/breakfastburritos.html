Breakfast Burritos

Having these first thing in the morning is the most perfectly balanced way to start the day.


Breakfast Burritos in the morning

Ingredients List (per serving):

  • 10g Spreadable Goat Cheese
  • 1 Tortilla
  • 2 Eggs
  • Salt & Pepper to taste
  • 10g Lettuce
  • (Optional) Fresh Aromatics

The recipe is as follows...

  1. Start by whisking the eggs
  2. Add eggs into a pan with olive oil
  3. Add salt & pepper to the eggs
  4. Fold in the eggs until scrambled
  5. Put the eggs aside
  6. Wet the tortilla and place it in the pan on low heat
  7. Spread the goat cheese & add spring onions
  8. Assemble the burrito on a seperate plate and put it back in the pan to sear
  9. Slice and Serve!
