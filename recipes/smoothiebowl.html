Ingredients List (per serving):

For the Base
  • 100g Frozen Strawberries
  • 1/2 Frozen Banana
  • 1 Scoop of Protein Powder or Yogurt
  • Salt & Pepper to taste
  • ? Water
Possible Toppings
  • Oatmeal
  • Goji Berries
  • Nut butter or nuts
  • Honey
  • Granola/Cereals
  • Chia seeds
Helpful tips:

Blend the base ingredients
If your blender isn't strong, chop the fruits before blending
Be sure not to add too much water for best results
